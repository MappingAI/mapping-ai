# Research Questions

Questions to explore in the Mapping AI insights page. These guide the analyses and visualizations.

---

## Guiding Principles

1. **Avoid selection bias** — focus on in-group comparisons rather than claiming "x% of people believe this" and extrapolating to the broader population
2. **Prioritize relational/structural analysis** — comment on what the ecosystem structure looks like and how actors move through the space, rather than counting beliefs
3. **Create follow-up opportunities** — surface questions, data sources, and avenues for deeper investigation

---

## 1. Belief Space (Regulatory Stance, AGI Timeline, Risk Level)

- Within each subset of map entries, which areas of the axis space are most/least dense? Are there heavily oversubscribed or empty portions? What is the significance?
- Are two of the three axes more correlated than the third? Does regulatory stance correlate with risk level better than with AGI timeline?
- Is there clustering within the distributions? Can we identify polarization within the discourse, or is the distribution fairly continuous?

---

## 2. Network Structure & Connectivity

- What is the breakdown of inbound vs outbound mentions by stakeholder category? Can we trace the progression of ideas through the ecosystem? (Prior assumption: frontier lab → think tank → gov/policy)
- How connected is each category to other categories? (Variations: stakeholder type, people vs orgs, ideological category)
- Who are the most/least connected stakeholder groups or ideological camps? Which groups function as bridges? Which are siloed?
- Which possible coalitions or arrangements of camps are most/least connected?
- Within each ideological category, what is the relative level of internal connectivity?
- Are specific resources or resource categories more/less connected with specific camps/orgs?

---

## 3. Threat Models & Key Concerns

- Which concerns are most/least likely to be cited together?
- How do specific groupings of concerns align with ideological camps?

---

## 4. Organizational Ecology

- Are there through-lines between funding model and ideological stance?
- Can we substantiate that belief distributions are structurally determined (geography, culture, etc.)? What is the geographic concentration within each ideological camp or stakeholder category?
- What do parent/subsidiary relationships look like? Can we quantify belief alignment between investors and their portfolio companies?
- *(Skipping: timelines/center of gravity — no founding date data)*

---

## 5. Data Quality & Submission Patterns

- Is there a difference in how submission type (1st person vs 3rd party) manifests in the data?
- Are specific categories of people more prone to certain submission types?
- *(Skipping: variance scores, transparency metrics — not yet computed)*
